Meet AutoClose

  1. Download the zip file and extract it
  2. type in chrome://extensions in your address bar
  3. Turn on developer mode, using toggle button on the top right
  4. Click "Load Unpacked" from top left corner and select the extracted zip file
  5. Go to any google meet session, and join
  6. Click the AutoClose button on the top right corner
